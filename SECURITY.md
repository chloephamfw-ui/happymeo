### V. ðŸ›¡ï¸ Vulnerability Reporting

We take security issues seriously and appreciate the efforts of security researchers who responsibly report vulnerabilities.

#### How to Report a Vulnerability

1.  **Do NOT** open a public GitHub issue.
2.  Report the issue privately by sending an email to our dedicated security mailbox: **security@[your-company-domain].com**
3.  Please encrypt your report using our PGP key. You can find our public key below in the Contact section (VII).
4.  Include the following information in your report:
    * **Description:** A clear and detailed description of the vulnerability.
    * **Location:** The affected component, file, or URL.
    * **Reproduction Steps:** Detailed steps (including screenshots or proof-of-concept code) required to reproduce the issue.
    * **Impact:** A summary of the potential security impact.
    * **Your Contact Information:** (Optional) If you wish to be credited.

#### Our Commitment

We commit to acknowledging all reports within **48 hours** and will keep the reporter informed of our progress. We will not take legal action against individuals who discover and report security vulnerabilities in accordance with this policy.
